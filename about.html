a creative at heart

Throughout high school, I was heavily interested in graphic design and the industry. Over that span of time, I honed my design skills and my understanding of visual communication. Wanting to explore what design meant in a more holistic scope, I expanded my learning to the UI/UX field. My interests have pivoted toward HCI and user-centered design, particuarly in direct manipulation interfaces and novel data representations to foster creative exploration and synthesis. Currently, I’m volunteering as a research assistant at The Design Lab at UC San Diego where I run user studies and carry out rapid prototyping. On the side, I work on illustration/graphic design projects (back to my roots), as well as scouting opportunities for free food on campus.
